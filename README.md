# 📊 Product Sales Data Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
Businesses must maintain detailed sales records to understand their financial performance and make informed decisions. This project focuses on analyzing sales data to uncover insights related to profit, sales trends, and regional performance.

This Power BI dashboard provides a complete view of product sales performance, helping identify key trends, high-performing products, and profitable regions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Track monthly profit trends to identify seasonality
- Compare profit distribution across different countries
- Identify top-performing products and segments
- Visualize global sales distribution using maps

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Microsoft Sample Dataset (Financial Data)
- Contains:
  - Product details
  - Sales and profit data
  - Country/region information
  - Date (monthly/yearly)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Metrics
- Total Sales: 119M
- Total Units Sold: 1M
- Total Profit: 17M
- Profit Margin: 14%

### 🔹 Visualizations Used
- 📈 Line Chart → Monthly Profit Trend
- 📊 Bar Chart → Segment-wise Profit Distribution by Country
- 🌍 Map Visual → Profit by Country (Global View)
- 🧩 Treemap → Profit by Product
- 🍩 Donut Chart → Product Distribution by Country

### 🔹 Filters (Slicers)
- Year Filter
- Product Filter
- Segment Filter

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- France and Germany generate the highest profits
- Certain months show strong seasonal growth patterns
- Products like Paseo and VTT contribute significantly to overall profit
- Profit distribution varies across regions, indicating market opportunities
